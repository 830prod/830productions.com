830 Productions Legal

Policies, terms, and usage rights.

This page covers legal terms for project services and digital shop products provided by 830 Productions.

Legal Document Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Scope Of Services

830 Productions provides photography, videography, editing, and digital product services as outlined in the approved quote, booking confirmation, or Stripe checkout details.

2. Payment Terms

  • Digital products are paid in full at checkout through Stripe.
  • Custom service projects may require deposits before production begins.
  • Outstanding balances must be paid before final delivery unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3. Cancellations And Rescheduling

Rescheduling requests are handled based on availability. Late cancellations may forfeit deposits or incur rebooking fees depending on project commitments already made.

4. Delivery And Revisions

Delivery timelines are estimates based on project complexity. Revision rounds are limited to what is defined in the project agreement or product terms.

5. RAW Files And Source Assets

RAW files and unedited source assets are not included by default. They may be licensed separately through a written RAW/source-file buyout agreement.

6. Usage Rights

  • Clients receive usage rights according to the package or agreement purchased.
  • 830 Productions retains portfolio rights unless an exclusivity or NDA term states otherwise.
  • Resale, redistribution, or repackaging of shop products is prohibited unless explicitly licensed.

7. Limitation Of Liability

830 Productions is not liable for indirect or consequential damages. Liability is limited to fees paid for the affected service or product.

8. Contact

For legal, licensing, or policy questions, contact 830productions@gmail.com.
